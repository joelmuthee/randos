About Us

Built on precision,
driven by expertise

Randos Consult Limited is an Electrical and Mechanical Engineering Services Consultancy Firm with registered offices in Nairobi, Kenya. Our qualified engineers bring extensive training and hands-on experience to every project.

Fully equipped with licensed design software and advanced testing and energy monitoring equipment, we deliver consistently excellent results across Kenya, East Africa and Central Africa.

Vision

To be the first-choice construction and property services consultancy — improving the built environment across East Africa.

Mission

To provide Innovative, Quality and Effective design solutions that consistently exceed client expectations.
